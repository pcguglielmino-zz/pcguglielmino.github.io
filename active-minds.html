Active Minds President

Leading for a cause

Throughout my life mental health has been important to me. That is why I joined Active Minds, the club on campus dedicated to raising awareness about mental health and suicide prevention. As a sophomore I ran to be Vice President of the organization and was elected, which put me in charge of all of the internal affairs of the club. As a result of my changes to how general body meetings were run I saw an improvement in member satisfaction and turnout, as measured by end of meeting surveys. These surveys also allowed for me to have feedback on the changes I was making and ensured that the members of the club were getting their needs met.
