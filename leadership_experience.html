Overview of Leadership experience

The things that I have learned from listening to others

I have always enjoyed working in teams, especially the dynamics of working with your close friends. I can vividly recall moments where I wanted to throw in the towel for the day on a project, but my friend helped push me to finish the problem that day, and then later in the evening I was able to return the favor.

Why I lead

As a result of my enjoyment of being on teams, learning from my teammates and taking their input, I often find myself as a team lead. This is something that I enjoy, and I take pride in. I enjoy being able to talk to people, learn their strengths, and then be able to assign them to work on things that they are excited about. Because I enjoy being a team lead, I find myself as an exec in many organizations around campus, and I have been able to learn valuable skills and have seen actual positive impact and growth from decisions I have made.
