Overview of Project Work

The things that I have learned through hard work

Throughout my education at Worcester Polytechnic Institute I have worked on many projects and developed a variety of skills. This was driven by the style of education provided by WPI. Because everything I learn I am expected to be able to do, I reinforce the skills I learn in class. I also surrounded by a wealth of knowledge and opportunities provided by my peers at WPI.

Where I learn from

I have developed a multitude of skills from my time at WPI, some of my skills come from project work, and some come from working with other students and listening to what they have to say. For example, I learned how to use AWS from my software engineering class where we created a website. I also gained skills in developing electric vehicles from a friend who wanted my help to make an electric snowboard. I also developed skills through personal interest, such as manufacturing, resulting in me building a plasma cutter for the machine shop on campus.
